Performance Materials

We utilize engineering-grade composites and polymers designed for high stress, heat deflection, and impact resistance.

Carbon Fiber Composite

Extreme tensile strength and stiffness. Perfect for functional gears, automotive brackets, and load-bearing parts.

Industrial ABS

High heat resistance and durability. The industry standard for impact-resistant housing and components.

Flexible TPU Elastomer

Rubber-like elasticity with high tear resistance. Ideal for seals, gaskets, and vibration dampening.

High-Impact Polymer

Smooth surface finish with mechanical properties similar to injection-molded plastics. Best for visual models and snap-fits.
